About Moi's Bridge Animal Feeds

Located in the heart of Kenya's agricultural hub, Moi's Bridge Animal Feeds is committed to providing premium nutrition solutions for livestock farmers across the region. With years of experience in animal nutrition, we understand the unique challenges faced by Kenyan farmers.

Why Choose Us?

  • Quality Assurance: All our feeds are formulated with premium ingredients and rigorous quality control.
  • Expert Formulation: Our feeds are designed by nutrition experts for optimal livestock health and productivity.
  • Local Availability: Conveniently located in Moi's Bridge, serving Uasin Gishu, Trans Nzoia, and surrounding counties.
  • Competitive Pricing: We offer the best value for money without compromising on quality.
  • Farmer Support: Our team provides expert advice on feeding programs and livestock management.
